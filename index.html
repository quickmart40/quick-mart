Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Simply browse products, add items to your cart, click on the cart icon, proceed to checkout, fill in your delivery details, and choose your payment method (UPI or Cash on Delivery).
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM). Cash on Delivery (COD) is coming soon!
How long does delivery take?
We deliver across India within 3-7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities usually receive orders within 2-3 days.
Can I cancel or return my order?
Yes! You can cancel your order before it is shipped. For returns, we offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Please check your order status in "My Orders" section.
Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. We never store your UPI PIN or banking passwords. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.
How can I track my order?
After placing an order, you can track its status in the "My Orders" section. We update the status at every stage: Pending, Confirmed, Shipped, and Delivered.
Do you offer free delivery?
Yes! We offer free delivery on all orders above Rs.500. For orders below Rs.500, a nominal delivery charge of Rs.40 is applied.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us via email at quimkmart098@gmail.com or call us at +91 9817205542. Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9AM to 9PM.
