Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details, choose a payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation via email.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI payments (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), credit/debit cards, net banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD). All online payments are processed through secure, encrypted gateways.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery (available in select cities) delivers within 24-48 hours. Free delivery on all orders above Rs. 499.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day easy return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can initiate a return from your orders page. The product must be unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order in real-time from the "My Orders" page. Each order shows the current status and progress. You'll also receive SMS and email updates at every stage.
Is there a warranty on products?
Yes, all electronics come with a brand warranty (6-24 months depending on the product). Fashion and other items have a 7-day replacement guarantee against manufacturing defects.
How do I contact customer support?
Our customer support is available 24/7. You can reach us via the WhatsApp button on the website, email us at support@quickmart.com, or call our toll-free number 1800-123-4567.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placing them, provided they haven't been processed yet. Go to "My Orders" and click on the order to see available actions.
